Partner Application

How to Join Our Network

We maintain a small, intentional network of professionals — because quality matters more than quantity. If you believe you meet our standard, we invite you to apply.

01
Submit Your Application

Complete the application form including your category, service area, credentials, and a brief description of your work and client focus. Include any relevant reviews, portfolio, or case studies.

02
Team Review

Our leadership team reviews every application personally. We look for demonstrated expertise in the luxury segment, evidence of client satisfaction, proper licensing and insurance, and a track record of discretion and professionalism.

03
Introduction Meeting

Qualified applicants are invited to a brief meeting with a member of our team — either in person or via video call — to discuss your practice, our clients' typical needs, and how we can best work together.

04
Network Acceptance

Accepted partners are featured on our Preferred Partners page, receive direct referrals from our team, and are introduced to our clients as needed. Partnership is reviewed annually based on client feedback and service quality.

05
Ongoing Standards

We monitor partner performance through direct client feedback. Partners who consistently deliver exceptional service remain in the network indefinitely. Those who don't meet our standard are removed — no exceptions, no referral fee influence.
