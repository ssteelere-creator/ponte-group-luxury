Probate & Estate

Court-Qualified
Estate Sales

Probate real estate is not a standard transaction. It involves court oversight, beneficiary dynamics, strict documentation requirements, and timelines governed by the probate process — not market preference. Our team understands these constraints and works within them seamlessly, protecting every party while delivering the best possible outcome for the estate.

We work directly with probate attorneys, personal representatives, executors, and court-appointed administrators across southeastern Massachusetts and Rhode Island. Our documentation, communication, and court-required procedures are built into every estate engagement from day one.

Court-compliant pricing, documentation, and disclosure procedures
Direct liaison with probate attorneys and court administrators
Full BPO / comparative market analysis for court submission
Overbid and counter-offer management in accordance with court requirements
Coordination with estate cleanout, staging, and repair vendors
Estate property management during the probate period if needed
Multi-beneficiary communication — clear, documented, and professional
Experience with contested estates and complex family situations
