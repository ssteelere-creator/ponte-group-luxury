About Alexis

Alexis joined The Ponte Group with a background that positioned her perfectly for luxury real estate: a professional history of high-touch client relationships, a genuine instinct for presentation and detail, and an authentic love for the communities she serves. She earned her KW Luxury International certification early and has not looked back since.

As Team Leader of the Luxury Division, Alexis manages the client experience from first contact through closing — coordinating showing schedules, managing offer strategy, overseeing listing preparation, and ensuring that every client feels fully informed and genuinely supported at every stage of what is, for most people, the largest financial transaction of their lives.

Her specialty is the coastal and lifestyle luxury segment — properties where location, views, and quality of life are as important as square footage and finishes. She knows Westport's waterfront, Bristol's historic harbor district, Portsmouth's Aquidneck Island estates, and Dartmouth's golf and marina communities with the depth of someone who has walked hundreds of properties in each and paid attention every time.

Alexis is also the primary author of The Ponte Group's neighborhood guides and market commentary — a direct reflection of the research depth and community knowledge she brings to every client conversation. When you work with Alexis, you work with someone who has genuinely done the homework, and who shares it openly, completely, and without reservation.
