About This Property

A rare and extraordinary waterfront estate set on 2.4 private acres along the Westport River — one of the most sought-after positions on the southeastern Massachusetts coastline. This meticulously maintained five-bedroom residence offers panoramic water views from every principal room, a level of privacy that is increasingly difficult to find, and the kind of uncompromising quality that defines true luxury at this price point.

The main residence spans 5,640 square feet across three floors, anchored by a chef's kitchen featuring professional-grade Wolf and Sub-Zero appliances, custom cabinetry, and a large center island that opens seamlessly to the main living areas. The primary suite occupies the entire second floor east wing, with a private terrace, two walk-in closets, and a spa bath finished in Calacatta marble. Four additional en-suite bedrooms provide ample space for family and guests.

The grounds are a destination in their own right — a private deep-water dock, a heated infinity pool with cabana, established perennial gardens, and 280 feet of waterfront footage. A three-car heated garage, whole-home generator, and fully integrated smart home system complete this exceptional offering. Rarely does a property of this character and position come to market in Westport.
