About Scott

Scott joined The Ponte Group with a clear mandate: build the infrastructure that transforms a strong real estate team into a truly elite one. The systems, platforms, and operational discipline that now power the Luxury Division are largely his creation — from the Follow Up Boss CRM architecture to the recruiting intelligence tools that help the team grow with intention.

His approach to real estate is data-first and strategy-driven. Where other agents lead with instinct, Scott leads with market analytics, pricing models, and negotiation frameworks built from hard evidence. His clients consistently describe the same experience: fully informed at every step, never pressured, and genuinely prepared for what came next.

In the $1M+ market, Scott specializes in waterfront estates, investment-grade luxury, and complex transactions — estate sales, probate, REO, and transactions where precision in process is as important as precision in price. He manages the team's institutional relationships and is the primary architect of the division's strategy and growth.

If you want the clearest, most honest picture of where the market is and exactly what to do about it — Scott is your call.
