About Cliff

Cliff launched The Ponte Group from a single belief: that deep local knowledge and genuine long-term relationships would always outperform a high-volume, transactional approach to the business. The track record across nearly 600 transactions has proved him right, and the probate attorneys, lenders, and fiduciaries who refer work to him year after year are the most honest measure of that proof.

As Broker-Owner, Cliff is the institutional memory of the team. He has seen every market cycle, negotiated through every condition, and built the professional relationships the team relies on daily. When a transaction gets complicated — legally, emotionally, or logistically — Cliff has almost certainly navigated something similar before.

His deepest expertise is in estate and probate sales, where his 18-year network of probate attorneys and court administrators in Bristol, Plymouth, and Newport Counties gives sellers and their legal counsel an advantage that newer agents simply cannot offer. He leads the team's most significant institutional relationships — with banks, servicers, and law firms who rely on The Ponte Group year after year because the quality of execution never varies.

Beyond transactions, Cliff is the cultural anchor of the team — the standard against which every agent, every interaction, and every outcome is measured. When you work with The Ponte Group, you are working inside a culture Cliff built deliberately, over nearly two decades.
