Who We Want

We Are Selective.
Intentionally.

The Ponte Group Luxury Division is not the right team for everyone — and that is exactly the point. We are building a team of professionals who raise the standard, not one that dilutes it.

Profile 01
The Experienced Producer

You have been in the business 3+ years, you are closing deals, but you have hit a ceiling with your current brokerage or team. You need better systems, stronger leadership, and a brand that reflects the level you are operating at.

Profile 02
The Luxury-Focused Agent

You have been working in or transitioning to the $1M+ market and understand that luxury real estate demands a different level of presentation, knowledge, and client management. You want to be part of a team that is known at that level.

Profile 03
The High-Potential New Agent

You are newer to the business but bring exceptional professional skills — sales background, marketing experience, strong local relationships, or prior high-level career experience — and you are willing to outwork everyone in the room to build something significant.

Profile 04
The Referral & Estate Specialist

You have a strong referral base, professional network, or specific expertise in estate, probate, or relocation — and you want to plug into a team with the infrastructure to amplify what you already do well.

What We Look For
Non-Negotiables
Licensed and in good standing in Massachusetts and/or Rhode Island
A commitment to professionalism — in appearance, communication, and conduct
Coachability — the ability to receive feedback, implement it, and grow from it
Accountability — ownership of results, good and bad, without excuses
A genuine commitment to client experience, not just the commission check
Willingness to participate in team culture — training, meetings, and collaboration
A track record that suggests real potential, even if volume is still developing
What Will Not Work Here
Agents who want a brokerage affiliation without genuine team engagement
A transactional mindset that prioritizes speed over client experience
Anyone unwilling to be coached or held to a production standard
Agents who treat compliance and ethics as optional
